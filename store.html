Great start, grat photos About Jay Paxton Photos

I have always grown up around cars, growing up me and my dad would fix cars, our own, friends, and neighbors cars. This gave me a love for automobiles and their design. I started car photography while I was in High school. I originally was into landscape photography but decided i would go out and take pictures of my old 1998 s10. I ended up posting these pictures on instagram and i started getting requests from friends and eventually complete strangers to take pictures of their cars and i have loved it ever since.

Up above is a photo of my dads 1972 corvette. He loves this car and so do I. My dad bought this car when it was a total wreck, wires everywhere, interior a mess. He worked on it replaced just about everything, got a custom interieor and upgraded parts of the motor while leaving as much of it stock as possible. This car signifies my dads hard work and his love for automobiles.
